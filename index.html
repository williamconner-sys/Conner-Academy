Our Pillars

Virtus — Virtue

We train habits of character every day: honesty, diligence, courage, gratitude, and service. Scholarship matters, yet who we become as we learn matters more.

Sapientia — Wisdom

Mastery-based learning with living books, Socratic discussion, and purposeful making. We pursue understanding by reading deeply, asking incisive questions, and explaining ideas with clarity in speech and writing.

Concordia — Harmony

We prize peace in our halls and joy in our studies. We work as one community, keep deliberate rhythms, and cultivate friendship, respect, and responsibility.

Far Above the Standard

We integrate classical humanities with modern science and technology, studio projects with field and nature study, and physical training with outdoor education. The result is depth, fluency, and character.

  • Mathematics with reasoning, problem solving, and proof
  • Great books with narration, composition, and rhetoric
  • Laboratory science with research methods and nature journals
  • World languages with conversation, culture, and literature
  • Computer science, robotics, engineering design, and data
