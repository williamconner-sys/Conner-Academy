Our Pillars

Virtus — Virtue

We practice habits of character every day: honesty, diligence, courage, gratitude, and service. Schoolwork is important, but who we become while doing it matters more.

Sapientia — Wisdom

Mastery-based learning, living books, discussion, and making. We seek understanding—reading deeply, asking good questions, and explaining ideas clearly in speaking and writing.

Concordia — Harmony

Peace in our home and joy in our studies. We work as a family team, keep rhythms that fit our household, and cultivate friendship, respect, and responsibility.

Far Above the Standard

We blend classical humanities with modern science and technology, hands-on projects with nature study, and physical wellness with outdoor education.

  • Math with reasoning and proofs as skills develop
  • Great books & narration, composition, and rhetoric
  • Laboratory science and nature journaling
  • World languages with conversation and culture
  • Computer science, robotics, and design
