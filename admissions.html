Admissions

We seek mission‑fit families who share our high expectations and joyful culture.

  1. Inquiry: Submit our interest form to receive timelines and events.
  2. Visit: Attend a campus tour or preview day.
  3. Apply: Application, records, recommendations, and student visit.
  4. Placement: Grade‑level assessments and interviews.
  5. Decision: Enrollment offer and onboarding.
