Admissions

We welcome mission-fit families who share our high expectations, strong character, and joyful culture.

How to Apply

  1. Inquiry: Email admissions for our information packet and key dates.
  2. Conversation: A consultation to confirm fit and answer questions. Virtual options available.
  3. Application: Application form, academic records, and recommendations.
  4. Placement: Grade-level assessments and a parent-student interview. Virtual options available.
  5. Decision: Enrollment offer with onboarding timeline.
Request Information Ask a Question

What We Look For

  • Curiosity, diligence, and kindness
  • Strong readiness in reading, writing, and mathematics
  • Commitment to family partnership and clear routines
  • Respect for our honor code and community standards

Tuition & Affordability

Conner Academy is committed to access. Indexed tuition and merit awards are available and consider academic promise and family circumstances.

For privacy and security we do not host on-site tours or open houses.
