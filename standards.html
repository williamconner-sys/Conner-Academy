Honor Code and Community Standards

We learn with integrity, respect, and responsibility. These standards guide our daily life together.

Honor Code

Students at Conner Academy live with integrity. We tell the truth, do our own work, give proper credit, care for people and property, and strengthen the good name of our school.

  • Honesty in word and deed
  • Academic integrity without cheating or plagiarism
  • Respectful speech and conduct
  • Responsibility for materials and shared spaces
  • Courage to correct mistakes and make amends

Community Standards

We keep a safe, orderly, and joyful learning environment in every setting, including online work.

  • Prepared, punctual, and ready to learn
  • Phones and personal devices put away during study unless approved
  • Kind, clear communication with peers and adults
  • Responsible technology use and digital citizenship
  • Dress and appearance that are neat and appropriate to learning

Full policies and procedures are provided upon enrollment in the Family Handbook.
