Leadership

Leadership is service, clarity, and example. We teach with craft, care, and conviction.

Leadership Philosophy

We lead by aligning mission and daily practice. We set clear goals, coach with kindness, measure what matters, and model the scholarship and character we ask of students.

Clarity Consistency Craft Care

Roles and Responsibilities

Head of School

Stewards mission, safety, culture, and long range planning. Coaches faculty and partners with families.

Dean of Academics

Leads curriculum, assessment, and professional learning. Ensures mastery, depth, and coherence across grades.

Dean of Students

Supports habits, character, and care systems. Guides advisory, attendance, and student life.

Director of Arts & Athletics

Builds programs that develop skill, discipline, and joy in performance and sport.

Operations & Safety

Manages calendars, communication, records, and safety procedures.

Faculty

Design rich lessons, give clear feedback, and form students in virtue, wisdom, and harmony.

Faculty Profile

What We Value

  • Strong content knowledge and love of learning
  • Clear instruction and high expectations
  • Warmth, patience, and calm authority
  • Team mindset and steady communication

Professional Growth

  • Mentoring and lesson study
  • Observation with timely feedback
  • Shared planning and unit design
  • Workshops and certifications by need

Safeguarding

  • Background checks completed
  • Annual safety training
  • Clear boundaries and supervision
  • Emergency readiness and drills

Governance and Communication

  • Mission oversight and annual goals
  • Transparent policies in a Family Handbook
  • Regular progress updates and conferences
  • Responsive email within one business day
